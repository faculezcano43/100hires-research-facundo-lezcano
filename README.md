# 100Hires Research Project: B2B SaaS LinkedIn Strategy

# Overview
This repository contains a research project focused on identifying and analyzing key experts in **B2B SaaS LinkedIn organic content strategy**. The goal is to understand actionable growth tactics directly from industry practitioners.

# Methodology
* **Selection:** 10 experts were selected based on their active roles in B2B SaaS companies (Head of Growth, CMO, Marketing Manager).
* **Data Collection:** Manual curation of recent LinkedIn posts to extract real-world growth strategies and insights.
